Why use Carpool Buddy? Carpool Buddy allows you to find people to carpool with. By carpooling you have less stress commuting to and from work, save on gas money, get to utilize carpool lanes, save time in traffic, and have more free time if you are a rider. Additionally, you also help reduce pollution, traffic on the roads, and the need for parking. By connecting with people to carpool with you are also making new friends and connecting with people in your neighborhood.
