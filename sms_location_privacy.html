    Auto Dialer Privacy Policy








    Effective Date: March 12th, 2017

    Share My Location currently requests access to your contacts, location, sms, phone, media/storage, camera, and network access. This app uses these permissions locally.
    • Contacts permission is accessed in order to auto select a contact for the phone number input.
    • Location permission is accessed in order to show your current location.
    • SMS permission is accessed to send your location as an SMS message.
    • Media/Storage permission is accessed to locally store the information of your location.
    • Network Access is used as an alternative to GPS used to show your current location and weather information for your location.
    • Phone and Camera permissions are not accessed at this time.
    The main purpose of this app is to share your location. The developer and no other third party has access to the information stored in this app.
